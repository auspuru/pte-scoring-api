📚 How to Summarize Written Text

Master the PTE SWT task

📝 Task Overview

Read a passage and write a ONE sentence summary in 5-75 words.

Example
"The author opted for a significant lifestyle change by exchanging a city terrace for a farm cottage; however, he acknowledged the minor disadvantages of country living, and ultimately argued that this transition was a beneficial decision."

💡 Research-Based Tips (from your mock test data)
  • Smart Swaps: Change verbs/adjectives, KEEP nouns. "made a choice" → "opted for a change"
  • Perspective Shift: "I made..." → "The author opted..."
  • Connector Pattern: Always use "; however," or "; therefore," with semicolon
  • Content Nouns: Never change London, GDP, $4 trillion — these ARE your content score
  • Sweet spot: 35-55 words typically scores best
